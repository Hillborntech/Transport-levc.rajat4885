WE ARE Hillborn Technologies

THE LONDON ELECTRIC
VEHICLE COMPANY

 

Our roots were planted in 1908. A heritage which started in the professional taxi trade has seen us build vehicles with an unrivalled blend of performance, functionality and craftsmanship.

Today, we are transformed from a traditional vehicle manufacturer, into a modern electric vehicle company. Once only synonymous with London, we now operate a green global footprint; with a manufacturing facility and Headquarters in Ansty, England, and sales operations across Europe and key international markets.
